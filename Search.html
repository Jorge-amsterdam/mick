Welcome to our client's portal Login and upload your project.

Once you have logged in, you can upload your floorplans and your list of requirements. With list of requirements, we mean your wishes, how would you like to have the whole thing to look like. Please make sure that your floorplans are in jpeg format and good resolution. 300 dpi would be great. As far as the list of requirements is concerned, a word doc would be just fine. Once your docs are uploaded they will be shared with our community of designers.As soon as one of our designers picked your project we will let you know.If more than one designer is interested we will also inform you. If you have any questions you can always contact us. You are also welcome to share with us what makes you tick and inspires you. Go on Pinterest and pin some designs that you love.
